In 2017, we lauched a project caled the Storybook Initiative.

Our aim was to help promote a reading culture; using creative mediums to tell our stories, we handcrafted our books & gifts, and packaged our collection of items for sale in a product we called the #GiftboxBooks.

From the success of the StoryBook Initiative, we launched the Adventure Starts Now #ASN Campaign in 2018.

Our #ASN Campaign was focused on inspiring creativity & imagination in young children. We worked with parents and schools to create customised creative learning materials for children.

In 2019, we received a letter of recognition and appreciation from the Ministry of Education in Enugu State, Nigeria.

The Department of Education Resource Center, recognized our efforts in promoting learning & imagination, and recommended our storybooks as alternative learning aids for children.

In 2021, we applied for our storybooks to become a part of the educational reading booklist in schools across Enugu state, Nigeria.

We are happy to announce that our bestselling storybook 'Hannah and the Farm' was approved into the 2021 booklist as a suiltable learning aid for primary school students.

To sustain our brand, and scale our impact across Africa, we launched Irakuri Hub in 2021.

We tested our idea; using the 'Storybook Initiative' and '#ASN', now we've rebranded ourselves into a registered business that's ready to disrupt the EDtech space.
